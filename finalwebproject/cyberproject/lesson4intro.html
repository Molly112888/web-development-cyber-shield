Botnets & Cyber Attacks

An introductory lesson on modern cyber attack techniques and defensive insights

About This Lesson

This lesson explains how modern botnets operate, how attackers exploit insecure devices, and how cyber attacks affect networks and individuals.

Learning Objectives

  • Explain what a botnet is and how it operates.
  • Identify common botnet‑based attacks such as DDoS.
  • Understand how IoT devices are compromised.
  • Recognize escalation from cyber attacks to real‑world harm.
  • Apply basic defensive strategies.

Key Topics Covered

  • Botnet infrastructure
  • DDoS and email flooding
  • IoT exploitation
  • Proxy abuse
  • Defense and prevention

Intended Audience

Designed for students and beginners seeking a practical understanding of modern cyber attacks.
